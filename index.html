There are certain advantages to hiring a candidate with a writing background. As Jason Fried, co-founder of Basecamp (creators of Ruby on Rails), put it:

“Hire the better writer… That's because being a good writer is about more than writing. Clear writing is a sign of clear thinking. Great writers know how to communicate.
